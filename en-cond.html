Condors hill and La Pajcha.

Tours:

1 to 2 days.

Location:

South of Samaipata.

Access Time:

2 hours in car.

Walk time:

2 hours on the way and 2 on the way back.

Altitude:

The walks are on trails from 1,400 to 2,100 meters above sea level.

Terms:

Take mountain boots or good shoes, in addition to hats and sunscreen.

Recommendable:

Recommended walk for people between 15 to 55 years old.

Fact:

More than 95% of the time you walk through more or less narrow paths discovered of arboreal vegetation.

This tours is only possible if it is not cloudy.

Description:

Condors hill is a circuit established just inside the most important condors movement route.

Here, you can observe male, female or juvenile condors every day of the year, from 3 individuals at a minimum to more than 50 in less than two hours.

On this route, there is the only drinking fountain and well-known bath in all of South America, which on hot days many condors are concentrated in a spectacular way to drink and bathe in a cliff-like rock more than 600 meters high where a small stream of water flows forming a waterfall, that more or less to the half of the 600 m of cliff, the rock forms a bowl that retains enough water to bathe several condors at the same time.
